Design is Sebastian's greatest passion. He loves many areas of life. He is
an avid basketball fan, he loves to paint abstract artwork, he loves to listen
to and play folk music on his guitar (not very well I may add), and he loves
memes as much as most other people in gen z.

But even with all these passions...

Art and Design are still number one. He loves learning about design and
experiencing it out in the world. His interests reflect back onto his ability
to design, and they are fundamental to who he is as a person and what he is
capable of designing.
